Our Background

Magezi was started as a small startup in 2019 during the covid pandemic. It was majorly aiming at providing cheap and conservative energy sources that would keep the environment clean.

Its major objective was transforming waste material like dry maize combs and banana peelings into a new state of renewable energy

The business has grown throught the past years as it has now expanded by market and production rate.
